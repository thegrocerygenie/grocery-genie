FAQ

The honest answers.

When does it launch?+

V1 — receipt scanning, price tracking, household sharing — ships Q3 2026 on iOS. The full pantry & meal-planning experience follows in Q4. Android comes after iOS hits 10K weekly active.

Will my receipts be private?+

Yes. Receipts are processed on-device for OCR, then encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or share your data. Anonymized, aggregated price points may inform the community-pricing feature — and that's fully opt-in.

What does it cost?+

Free forever for receipt scanning, ledger, basic budgets, and 3-month price history. Premium ($3.99/mo) unlocks the full price history, cross-store comparison, alerts, exports, and recipe matching. Family ($6.99/mo) covers up to five household members.

Does it work with email receipts?+

Yes. Forward digital receipts from Amazon, Instacart, Whole Foods, etc. to a private inbox and they're parsed automatically. Photo and paper receipts work too.

Why "Genie"?+

Because the best kitchen tool is the one that does the work for you and stays out of the way until you ask. Less spreadsheet, more wish-granting.
